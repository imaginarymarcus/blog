Brian Milotte Time Out

He let the shears drop to the ground with a loud clang and began to circle her, eyeing her up and down. His stoic expression gave away none of his underlying emotions as he inspected his prize. She felt like a diver, at risk of being devoured by a massive shark. For an instant, she longed for the safety of the cage. As if reading her mind, he stopped pacing and breathed a gruff order into her ear. “Get in.”
